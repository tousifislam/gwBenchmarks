Metrics

PHYSICS-MOTIVATED

Waveform & Analytic

output: Re(h₂₂), Im(h₂₂)
Mean frequency-domain mismatch over total masses [40, 80, 120, 160, 200] M☉ using aLIGO ZeroDetHighPower PSD, f_low=15 Hz, f_high=990 Hz, maximised over time and phase.

Remnant

output: kick velocity |v_k|
Normalised RMSE: NRMSE(v_k) = RMSE / range(v_k*). Dimensionless and robust to the scale of kick velocities across the parameter space.

Dynamics

output: x(t)
Pointwise RMS relative error: √(mean((x̂−x*)²/x*²)). Weights all time steps equally in fractional terms, avoiding late-time bias from the monotonically growing x(t).

Ringdown

output: Re(ω), Im(ω)
Mean of per-mode relative errors: (|ΔωR/ωR*| + |ΔωI/ωI*|) / 2. Evaluated on the (ℓ=2, m=2) fundamental mode; best model tested on higher modes.

Validity

output: predicted mismatch M̂
RMSE in log₁₀ space: RMSE(log₁₀ M̂, log₁₀ M*). Appropriate because mismatches span orders of magnitude (~10⁻⁷ to ~10⁻¹).

General rules

no scoring penalty
All metrics are raw accuracy losses — no runtime penalty. Agents must try ≥ 20 modelling approaches across 4 categories (kernel/GP, symbolic regression, interpolation, ML). PySR and gplearn are mandatory.
