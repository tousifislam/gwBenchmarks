Waveform Benchmark Metric Audit (2026-05-02)
=============================================

Goal: Verify all 10 LLM agents use identical metric and validation set for
the waveform benchmark before reporting in the paper.

Canonical metric
----------------
  Function:  gwbenchmarks.metrics.mean_fd_mismatch
  Backend:   PyCBC match (pycbc.filter.match)
  PSD:       aLIGOZeroDetHighPower
  Masses:    [40, 80, 120, 160, 200] M_sun  (mean over 5 evaluations)
  f_low:     15 Hz
  f_high:    990 Hz
  Match:     Maximised over time shift and orbital phase
  Output:    1 - match, in [0, 1]
  Val set:   250 waveforms
